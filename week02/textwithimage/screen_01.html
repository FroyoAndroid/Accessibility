Organization of the Body; Musculoskeletal System
In this lesson, you will begin to think about the body and its structures in terms of how it is organized – how parts of body are grouped according to function, where each part is located in relation to other parts or positions in the body, and how we use specific terminology to describe locations in the body.
Then, you will examine the terminology of the musculoskeletal system, the first of the body systems we will look at in detail. The major bones and muscles of the body will be covered, as well as some common pathologies, laboratory tests, and diagnostic procedures.
Lastly, you will have an opportunity to hear the pronunciation of the terms you have learned.
